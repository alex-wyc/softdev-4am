Time of Candidates' Tweets

About

It is said every election year that "this is the election of the social media." A smart media team is an invaluable asset for any candidate, and a savvy twitter user can reach a lot of voters by the simple act of typing less than 140 characters and clicking a button. However, it is interesting to see when the current political candidates tweet their tweets. This graph is based on the past 200 tweets of each of the candidates, and demonstrates their distribution across the hours of the day.
